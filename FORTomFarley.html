CST Multi-Asset 24-Hour Fractal Forecast

Heads means the next hour closes above the current price. Tails means it closes below. The model now generates its own 24-hour volatility path. It samples completed historical segments, reflects some of them backward, refracts them forward through the current heads/tails bias, and repeatedly stretches or compresses their amplitude. The result is an up-and-down, self-similar forecast that grows and shrinks like a fractal instead of using one purple curve.
